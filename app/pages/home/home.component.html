Diploma

Diploma Course are short-term courses that are of 1-3 year duration and can be pursued after completion of Classes 10 and 12. The admission to diploma courses is done either through entrance exam or direct admission.

Engineering

Candidates who have qualified in the MHT CET or JEE Main are eligible for the Maharashtra B.Tech admission 2022 subject to fulfilling the MHT CET eligibility criteria.
